Who
we are
Since the first days of the war, the "Save UA" Charity Fund has been actively helping Ukrainian civilians, providing them with food and hygiene products. The organization also supports hospitals, volunteers and citizens who became victims of Russian aggression in Ukraine.
our
goals
We created a fund so that caring people from all over the world could contribute to helping the Ukrainian people. We personally purchase all the necessary resources, deliver them to people, and then send a report to our volunteer angels.

We provide immediate and continuous first aid to vulnerable women, children and displaced persons, so we need support in the procurement of medicines, warm clothes, diapers, food and logistics.
Donate now

During the period of our work we :

  • We provided about 300 tons of humanitarian aid to the residents of Chernihiv Oblast
  • Organized two charity festivals in Vienna together with KOL Maximiliana
  • We donated $4,500 for a 9-year-old boy who was injured by a rocket
  • More than 400 aid packages were delivered
  • We provided 748 kg of meat for the consumers
  • we provided more than 20 tons of assistance to animals
  • Over 100 children's care packages were provided
